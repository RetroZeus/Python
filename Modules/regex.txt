RegEx or Regular Expressions are used as a search pattern for a sequence of text.
All tools use a search use the speciality of RegEx

. - Any character except New Line
\d - Digits (0-9)
\D - Not a digit (0-9)
\w - All word character (a-z),(A-Z),(0-9)
\W - Not a word character
\s - searches for white space (space,tab,newline)
\S - Not whitespace (space,tab,newline) 

\b - Word boundary
\B - Not word boundary 
^ - Beginning of a string
$ - End of a string

[] - Matches characters in brackets
[^ ] Matches characters not in brackets
| - Either,Or
() - Group
